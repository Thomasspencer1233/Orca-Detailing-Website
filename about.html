Where it began

How It Started

"It started in a driveway. In middle school. With a bucket, some soap, and an obsession with clean."

Most kids in middle school are worried about homework or video games. I was outside detailing cars. It started simply — a neighbor's car here, a family friend's SUV there — but from the first time I saw a dull, road-worn vehicle transform into something that looked fresh off the lot, I was hooked.

I reinvested every dollar back into better products and equipment. I learned everything I could about paint care, interior cleaning, and what it actually takes to do the job right — not just fast. Word spread through the neighborhood, then the town, then across the Upper Valley.

After over three years and more than 100 cars, Orca Detailing isn't just something I do — it's who I am. Every job gets the same care and attention as the very first one.

Year One · Middle School
THE FIRST DETAIL

It started with a single car and a curiosity about what was possible with the right products and some serious elbow grease. The results spoke for themselves — and so did the people who saw them.

BUILDING A REPUTATION

Referrals started coming in faster than I could keep up with. I upgraded my equipment, expanded the services I offered, and started treating every booking like a business — because it was becoming one.
